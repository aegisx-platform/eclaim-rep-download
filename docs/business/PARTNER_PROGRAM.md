## Partner Agreement

### Key Terms Summary

**Full legal agreement provided separately. Key highlights:**

#### 1. Term & Termination

- **Initial Term:** 1 year from effective date
- **Renewal:** Auto-renews for 1-year periods unless either party terminates with 60 days notice
- **Termination for Cause:** Either party can terminate immediately for:
  - Material breach not cured within 30 days
  - Bankruptcy or insolvency
  - Violation of IP or confidentiality

#### 2. Territory & Exclusivity

**Non-Exclusive (Default):**
- Partner can sell anywhere in Thailand
- Vendor can appoint multiple partners in same area
- No minimum sales quota

**Exclusive Territory (Optional):**
- Partner gets exclusive rights in defined region (e.g., Northeast Thailand)
- **Minimum Quota:** Must achieve 10 customers in Year 1, 20 in Year 2
- If quota not met, exclusivity removed
- Vendor can still do direct sales to named accounts

#### 3. Pricing & Commissions

**List Pricing:**
- Vendor sets standard list prices
- Partner can discount up to 15% without approval
- Larger discounts require vendor approval

**Commission Payment:**
- Partner invoices vendor monthly
- Payment within 30 days of vendor receiving customer payment
- Chargebacks if customer doesn't pay or cancels within 90 days

#### 4. Intellectual Property

- Vendor retains all IP rights to software
- Partner gets non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use/demonstrate software
- Partner cannot modify source code
- Partner branding/logos belong to partner

#### 5. Confidentiality

**Confidential Information Includes:**
- Product roadmap
- Pricing structures
- Customer data
- Source code
- Business strategies

**Obligations:**
- Protect confidential info with reasonable care
- Only use for partnership purposes
- Do not disclose to third parties
- Return/destroy upon termination

**Duration:** Survives for 3 years after termination

#### 6. Non-Compete

**During Term:**
- Partner cannot sell competing E-Claim automation products
- Exception: Existing products sold before partnership (must disclose)

**Post-Termination:**
- No non-compete restrictions after agreement ends
- But must return customers/data/materials

#### 7. Liability & Warranties

**Vendor Warranties:**
- Software performs substantially as documented
- Does not infringe third-party IP
- Will fix material defects within reasonable time

**Liability Limitations:**
- Vendor liability capped at fees paid in last 12 months
- No liability for indirect/consequential damages
- Partner responsible for their installation work

**Insurance:**
- Partner recommended to carry professional indemnity insurance (min 5M à¸šà¸²à¸—)

**Rule:** Customer belongs to whoever first engaged them

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Can I be both a partner and a direct customer?**
A: Yes, if your company operates multiple hospitals, you can buy licenses for your own facilities and also sell to others.

**Q: Do I need to be technical to become a partner?**
A: For Reseller/Agent, no. For Implementation Partner, yes - you need basic Linux/Docker skills or willingness to learn.

**Q: How long does it take to close a deal?**
A: Average sales cycle: 30-90 days. Smaller hospitals are faster (30-45 days), larger hospitals take longer (60-90 days).

### Pricing & Commissions

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**
A: You can discount up to 15% from list price. Larger discounts need vendor approval. You cannot price above list without customer's knowledge.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Within 30 days of vendor receiving customer payment. For invoice-split model, you get paid directly by customer.

**Q: What if customer doesn't pay?**
A: If customer defaults within 90 days, commission is charged back. After 90 days, you keep the commission.

### Territory & Competition

**Q: Do I get exclusive territory?**
A: Negotiable. Exclusive rights require minimum sales quota (typically 10 customers/year).

**Q: Can I compete with other partners?**
A: In non-exclusive territories, yes. Best partner wins based on relationship and service quality.

**Q: Can I sell outside Thailand?**
A: Requires separate agreement. Currently focused on Thai market.

### Technical & Support

**Q: What if I can't resolve a customer issue?**
A: Escalate to vendor L2 support via partner portal. We'll help resolve and use it as a training opportunity.

**Q: Do I need to keep inventory?**
A: No, this is software. We provide license keys electronically.

**Q: Can I customize the product for customers?**
A: Minor configurations yes (via settings). Custom development requires vendor involvement and separate agreement.

### Training & Certification

**Q: How often do I need to re-certify?**
A: Annually, or when major product updates are released.

**Q: Is training free?**
A: Initial 3-day training is free. Re-certification is free. Additional training sessions may have nominal fees.

**Q: Can I send multiple people to training?**
A: Yes, we recommend 1 sales + 1 technical person minimum.
